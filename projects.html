Previous Projects

Emoji Prediction

What's the last emoji? The use of emojis is more frequent than ever due to both the rapid growth of social media platforms and the increasing amount of available emojis. Hence, Gordon and I thought it would be fun to predict emojis in Twitter tweets given the (previous) words people use. Code is available here.

Cross-Domain Authorship Attribution

Authorship Attribution (AA) is the task of determining the author of a text from a set of candidates. This classification task is highly relevant for fields such as forensics or the detection of plagiarism. Read our paper here and look at the code here.
